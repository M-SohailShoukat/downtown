Our process is designed to make publishing your book smooth and efficient!

Print on Demand

Print-on-demand reduces financial risk, allows easy updates to existing titles, and eliminates out-of-stock issues without compromising quality. It also supports scalability, making it ideal for self-publishers aiming to boost book sales.

03
03
Self-Publishing

Self-publishing lets authors independently publish their work, reach their audience, and retain full control. It offers higher royalty rates and complete autonomy over the process, without needing traditional publishers.

02
02
E-Book Conversion

Self-publishing simplifies the process for authors, allowing them to publish and convert their books into e-books. A key step is e-book conversion, which ensures the book is readable across various platforms. The two main industry formats are ePub and .mobi.

04
04
ISBN Acquisition

For independent authors, obtaining an International Standard Book Number (ISBN) is essential for the publishing process. It helps booksellers, libraries, and readers identify and purchase the book.
